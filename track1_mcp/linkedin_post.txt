Exposing backend systems to these unvalidated loops risks massive regulatory liabilities under the EU AI Act (€35M) and India's DPDP Act.

Our latest architecture audit demonstrates the power of the Natoma Zero-Trust Proxy pattern:
- Interdicting path-traversal attacks at the ingress proxy adds a negligible 2ms of micro-latency.
- However, it saves the 1200ms of reasoning compute that would otherwise be wasted on high-cost GPU clusters before failing downstream.

Under active workloads, this edge-filtering model reduces downstream API costs by up to 98.4%.
